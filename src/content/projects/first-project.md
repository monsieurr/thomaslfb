## Overview
This internal tool was developed at MC2I to help consultants benchmark the carbon footprint of their client interventions.

## The Challenge
Most calculators focus on *cloud usage*. We needed to calculate the impact of *human intervention* (travel, devices) + *digital infrastructure*.
